PyART.waveform.waveform2energetics(h, doth, t, modes, mnegative=False)[source]¶

Compute GW energy and angular momentum from multipolar waveform See e.g. https://arxiv.org/abs/0912.1285

  • h[(l,m)] : multipolar strain

  • doth[(l,m)] : time-derivative of multipolar strain

  • t : time array

  • modes : (l,m) indexes

  • mnegative : if True, account for the factor 2 due to m<0 modes
